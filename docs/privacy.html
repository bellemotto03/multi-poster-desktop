Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Multi-Poster-Desktop ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. Multi-Poster-Desktop respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it.

1. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. Multi-Poster-Desktop operates entirely on your local machine to provide efficient management from your PC. The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • OAuth Tokens: Encrypted access tokens for API authentication, stored locally.
  • Account IDs: Non-sensitive identifiers for connected accounts.

2. How We Use Data

Information stored within Multi-Poster-Desktop is used solely to authenticate with official social media APIs and facilitate content management. No data is transmitted to any server other than the official API endpoints.

3. Data Sharing

Multi-Poster-Desktop does not sell, share, or transmit any user data to third parties. All credentials remain encrypted and stored locally on your device.

4. Data Security

OAuth tokens within Multi-Poster-Desktop are encrypted using Fernet symmetric encryption. The encryption key is stored locally and never transmitted elsewhere.

5. Third-Party APIs

Multi-Poster-Desktop interacts only with official APIs (e.g., TikTok Content Posting API). Please refer to each platform's own privacy policy for how they handle your data.

6. Your Rights

You may delete all data stored by Multi-Poster-Desktop at any time by removing accounts through the app or deleting the local configuration files.
